Dr. Hina Sajid is a Surgical Resident in Islamabad. She has scholarly interests center on translational research, especially the development of new minimally invasive surgical approaches to a wide variety of intra- and extra-cranial diseases. She has cleared her FCPS-I and now looking for 4 years of surgical residency in a reputed medical institution in Pakistan to practice general surgery. She is working under the direct supervision of top of line specialist surgeons who experts in their domains with years and years of experience. Write Medical Blogs, lead medical sessions as well as attend medical events and conferences as a speaker, presenter or panelist. She follows Dr. Fatima Jinnah, trying to work for the people like her to make her nation proud. Being a medical doctor she encourages her imagination every day.
